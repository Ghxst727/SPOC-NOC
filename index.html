The difference in colors represents the performance of the section.

For example:

Red: Your school is using 90% or more of its bandwidth. As a result, your internet speed may be slow or inconsistent.

Orange: Your school is using 80-90% of its bandwidth. This could mean slowness or interruptions.

Yellow: Your school is using 70-80% of its bandwidth.

Green: Your school is using 69% or less of its bandwidth.

If these colors are difficult to differentiate, clicking on the legends of the section will present it's status.
